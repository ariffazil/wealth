# 6. Why most indicators are not enough

Most chart indicators are descriptive, not predictive.
They summarize past behaviour.
They do not directly know:
- future liquidity,
- policy shocks,
- positioning asymmetry,
- narrative collapse,
- forced buying or forced selling.

The indicators that matter more are not RSI and MACD by themselves.
The variables that matter are:
- liquidity,
- regime,
- positioning,
- breadth,
- catalyst timing,
- structure.

So for this brief, price indicators are used only as support tools, not as gods.
