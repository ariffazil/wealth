- Yahoo Finance (via yfinance)
- World Bank economic indicators
- IMF financial statistics
- BIS banking data

## Energy
- EIA (US Energy Information Administration)
- IEA (International Energy Agency)
- ENTSO-E (European grid)
- JODI (Joint Organisations Data Initiative)

## Food
- FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization)
- USDA WASDE (World Agricultural Supply and Demand)
- IFPRI (International Food Policy Research)
- Satellite crop monitoring

## Water
- WRI Aqueduct (Water Risk Atlas)
- UN Water statistics
- GRACE (groundwater monitoring)
- River discharge gauges

## Notes
All data ingested into WEALTH must carry epistemic tags (CLAIM, PLAUSIBLE, ESTIMATE, UNKNOWN) per F2 and F9.
