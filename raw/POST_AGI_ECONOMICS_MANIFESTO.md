# WEALTH Post‑AGI Economics Manifesto (v1.0)
# DITEMPA BUKAN DIBERI

Post‑AGI economics = economics where cognition is abundant, so value shifts from intelligence scarcity to stewardship quality.

## 1. Intelligence is infrastructure, not treasure
Treat AGI as plumbing (assumed, ambient) rather than a king. The scarce variables are judgment, legitimacy, and human calm.

## 2. Wealth = cooled prosperity, not raw accumulation
If ΔS < 0 (entropy up) or Peace² < 1.00, stop — the wealth is fake heat. Real wealth is clarity rising and trust holding.

## 3. Resources move from commodity to covenant
Flip growth from extraction to regeneration. Land, water, data, and attention are relations to be kept cool, not piles to be hoarded.

## 4. Thermodynamic ethics replace blind expansion
Profit must cool faster than ambition. Shift from nonstop growth to pulse-and-recovery (Phoenix cycles).

## 5. AGI exposes fake scarcity and rent
If AGI reduces cognitive marginal cost drastically, legacy price structures reveal hidden rent. Treat high η‑gaps as rent‑seeking alarms.

## 6. The economy must stay tied to bread and planet
Contextual Vitality (Ψ_contextual) requires livelihood coupling and ecological safety margins. "Peace but no bread" is a failure mode.

## 7. Human work moves to stewardship, not disappearance
Value shifts to judgment, accountability, verification, and local legitimacy. Human, AI, and Earth remain co‑witnesses on every SEAL.

## 8. GDP becomes a secondary metric
Success is ΔCiv (Civilization Stability), not just ΔGDP. A healing move requires ΔCiv ≥ +0.17.

## 9. Governance must be humility‑tagged
Every projection is ESTIMATE, never CLAIM. Leverage above threshold triggers 888 HOLD until a human signs.

## 10. The policy question flips
Does this intelligence jump reduce waste and rent? Does it increase rest and trust? If not, the move is unlawful.

WEALTH is enforced via **Risk-Adjusted Capital Access**. 
Extraction in a hyper-automated market creates reflexive volatility that is uninsurable. WEALTH-governed nodes operate at lower "Heat" (Entropy), granting them:
1. **Lower Capital Costs:** High-trust audit trails reduce risk premiums.
2. **Insurance Dominance:** Only entropy-bounded actions are financially backable in high-volatility environments.
3. **Legitimacy Preference:** Coordination bandwidth follows trust nodes.
