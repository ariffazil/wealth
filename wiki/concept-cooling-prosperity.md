# Concept: Cooling Prosperity

> **"Money that calms, not burns."**

---

## Definition

In the arifOS frame, wealth stops being measured as accumulation and starts being measured as **cooled prosperity**:

- **Clarity rising**
- **Stress falling**
- **Trust holding**
- **Society becoming kinder**

## The Integrated Guide Rule

> If ΔS < 0 or Peace² < 1.00, stop.

Any capital that fails this rule is **fake heat** — it may look like growth on a spreadsheet, but it is increasing disorder in the human and social systems it touches.
