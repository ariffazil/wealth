# Concept: Post-AGI Economics

> Economics where cognition is abundant, so value shifts from intelligence scarcity to stewardship quality.

---

## Core Thesis

Post-AGI economics is not "an economy after smarter machines." It is an economy **redesigned because intelligence has become abundant, fast, and cheap enough to expose waste, false scarcity, and misaligned incentives**.

## What Changes

| Old Scarcity | New Scarcity |
|--------------|--------------|
| Raw cognition | Good judgment |
| Information | Legitimacy |
| Speed | Ecological room |
| Automation | Human calm |

## The 10-Point Manifesto

1. **Intelligence is infrastructure, not treasure.** Treat AGI as plumbing (assumed, ambient) rather than a king.
2. **Wealth = cooled prosperity, not raw accumulation.** If ΔS < 0 or Peace² < 1.00, stop.
3. **Resources move from commodity to covenant.** Flip growth from extraction to regeneration.
4. **Thermodynamic ethics replace blind expansion.** Profit must cool faster than ambition.
5. **AGI exposes fake scarcity and rent.** High η-gaps are rent-seeking alarms.
6. **The economy must stay tied to bread and planet.** Ψ_contextual requires livelihood coupling and ecological safety margins.
7. **Human work moves to stewardship, not disappearance.** Value shifts to judgment, accountability, verification, and care.
8. **GDP becomes a secondary metric.** Success is ΔCiv (Civilization Stability), not just ΔGDP.
9. **Governance must be humility-tagged.** Every projection is ESTIMATE, never CLAIM.
10. **The policy question flips.** Does this intelligence jump reduce waste and rent? Does it increase rest and trust?
