# Concept: Maruah

> **Maruah = Dignity.** A real financial variable in the arifOS/WEALTH stack.

---

## Definition

Maruah is the dignity and integrity score of a capital action or financial state. It is not a soft metric. It is **hardcoded into floor enforcement**.

## The 5 Dimensions

1. **Integrity** — Are all numbers honest and tagged?
2. **Sovereignty** — Is financial data under human control?
3. **Debt Dignity** — Is debt within honourable limits?
4. **Amanah** — Trust/stewardship signals (ASEAN/MY context)
5. **Community** — Zakat, sedekah, or equivalent contribution

## Maruah Score Calculation

```
score = (integrity + sovereignty + debtDignity + amanah + community) / 5
```

## Grading

| Score | Grade | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| > 0.90 | AAA_GRADE | Sovereign band |
| ≤ 0.90 | SUB_PRIME | Monitor / repair |
| < 0.60 | RED | 888 HOLD triggered |

## Constitutional Role

- **F6** (Maruah-First) requires ASEAN/MY dignity context.
- @WEALTH issues an **ABSOLUTE VETO** if dignity risk ≥ 0.40 and amanah is broken.
- The competitive moat of WEALTH is **"Maruah > Convenience"**.
