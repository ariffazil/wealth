# Concept: Contextual Vitality

> A system cannot claim success just because it is internally efficient. It must also preserve livelihoods and ecological ceilings.

---

## The Equation

$$
\Psi_{contextual} = \Psi_{internal} \times \lambda_p \times \lambda_e
$$

## Variables

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Ψ_internal | Internal system efficiency or intelligence score |
| λₚ | **Livelihood coupling** — does the system produce bread? |
| λₑ | **Ecological safety margin** — does it respect planetary boundaries? |

## The Warning

The canon explicitly flags the danger of **"peace but no bread."**

A system may be:
- Highly optimized
- Low entropy internally
- Peaceful in operation

And still be **unlawful** if it decouples from human livelihood or violates ecological boundaries.

## In WEALTH

This is why every 999 SEAL requires a **triple witness**:
- **Human** — livelihood and dignity
- **AI** — intelligence and optimization
- **Earth** — physical and ecological grounding (GEOX)
