# Concept: Stewardship Shift

> Resources are relations that must remain cool, not piles to be hoarded.

---

## The Paradigm Shift (ATLAS-33)

| Dimension | Old Logic | New Logic |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Resource | Commodity | Covenant |
| Growth | Extraction | Regeneration |
| Efficiency | Output / time | rest × clarity / stress |
| Ownership | Possession | Custodianship |

## Why Now?

In a post-AGI economy, intelligence is abundant. That means:
- The scarce resource is **not cognition**.
- The scarce resources are **good judgment, legitimacy, ecological room, and human calm**.

## Stewardship in Practice

- Land is held in trust for future generations.
- Data is governed by consent and dignity (F8).
- Attention is treated as a finite ecological resource.
- Capital circulates rather than accumulates in static pools.
