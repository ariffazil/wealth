# Concept: Proxy Efficiency Test

> When η ≪ 1, the "heat" in the system is waste, not real value creation.

---

## The Equation

$$
\eta_{proxy} = \frac{\text{Cost}_{\text{incumbent}}}{\text{Cost}_{\text{competitor}}}
$$

## Interpretation

When AGI can perform a high-value cognitive task at a tiny fraction of incumbent cost, legacy price structures reveal their true nature:

- **Friction** — bureaucratic overhead
- **Rent** — gatekeeping without service
- **Prestige markup** — price driven by status, not value
- **Institutional inertia** — outdated structures resisting efficiency

## Post-AGI Implication

Intelligence abundance does not just automate tasks. It **audits markets**. Sectors with η ≪ 1 are exposed as heat-producing rather than value-producing.

## Policy Response

In the WEALTH frame, a low η_proxy is not celebrated as "disruption." It is treated as a **rent-seeking alarm**. The policy question becomes: *Can this efficiency gain be redirected into circulation, livelihood, and ecological safety?*
