# capitalx — Risk Pricing Engine

> The missing fifth layer. Converts constitutional philosophy into actuarial advantage.

---

## Purpose

`capitalx` answers:

> **"What should this actor pay to borrow capital, given their entropy, dignity, peace, and civilization impact?"**

Without `capitalx`, WEALTH is moral philosophy.  
With `capitalx`, WEALTH becomes **sovereign infrastructure**.

## Core Thesis

> **Extractive actors pay more. Entropy-bounded actors pay less.**

When the cost-of-capital differential is real and measurable, markets migrate toward low-entropy nodes **without coercion**.

## Input Signals

| Signal | Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|--------|---------|
| dS | Entropy delta | Disorder injected |
| peace2 | Peace score | Stability index |
| maruahScore | Maruah | Dignity composite |
| trustIndex | Trust topology | Verified network strength |
| deltaCiv | ΔCiv | Long-horizon civilization stability |

## Pricing Formula

```
adjusted_rate = base_rate
                + entropy_penalty
                - peace_discount
                - maruah_discount
                - trust_discount
                - civ_discount
```

## API

- `calculateRiskAdjustedRate(baseRate, signals)`
- `compareCapitalAdvantage(baseRate, wealthSignals, extractiveSignals)`
